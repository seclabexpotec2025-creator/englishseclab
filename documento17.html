How to bake cookies

1) Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F) and line a baking sheet with baking paper.

2) Beat the butter and sugar in a large bowl until creamy.

3) Add the egg and vanilla and continue mixing until well combined.

4) Add the flour and salt little by little, mixing with a spatula or spoon until a smooth dough forms.

5) Add optional extras (chocolate chips, nuts, etc.) and mix gently.

6) Form the dough into balls with your hands and place them on the baking sheet, leaving space between them. You can flatten them slightly with your hand or a fork.

7) Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden.

8) Let cool for a few minutes on the tray, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
