Office 2021 Installation Guide

Prepare your computer and account

● Make sure you have a Microsoft account and that your computer meets the system requirements.

Get Office

● Get a Microsoft 365 subscription or an Office license.

Download the installer

● Go to office.com, sign in, and click Install Office.

Install the program

● Open the downloaded file (.exe on Windows or .pkg on Mac) and follow the on-screen instructions.

Activate and update

● Open any Office app, sign in or enter your product key, and then update to keep it secure and with the latest features.
